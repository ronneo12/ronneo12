The UX/UI Designer



As a UX/UI designer with more than two decades of experience, knowledge of web development, and unique perspective on how to create effective and user-friendly web interfaces that are both aesthetically pleasing and functional. I have also worked on a wide range of projects, from simple websites to complex web applications and banking transaction prototypes. Throughout my career, I have honed my skills in web UX/UI design, project management and a team leader that needs to collaborated with stakeholders across different departments, that enable me to deliver high-quality design solutions that meet the needs of my clients. As a corporate webmaster at Ngee Ann polytechnic, I gained invaluable experience in creating and maintaining corporate websites that effectively communicate the brand message and engage with my clients’ target audience.

My approach to UX/UI design is based on a deep understanding of users’ needs and behaviors. I work closely with the development team to create a design that is not only visually appealing but also easy to navigate. My understanding of importance consistency and the coherence across different digital touchpoints, and platform was to ensure a seamless user experience, I leverage my vast knowledge of web development to ensure that the design is function feasible and can be implemented efficiently.
