A Landscape Photographer



As a leisure travel landscape photographer, I have been to many places in Asia, like Japan, China, Philippine, Australia, Thailand, Malaysia, Indonesia, Hong Kong and New Zealand to take the landscape photographs that is unique to these countries.

Throughout my journey, I have not only captured stunning landscapes but also learned about different cultures, made many new friends, also captured a collection of beautiful photographs and unforgettable memories.

Landscape Photography


Philippine (Boracay)

In the Philippines, I have captured the breathtaking beaches and crystal-clear waters to captured the sunset over the famous Boracay Beach and went islands hoping.


Landscape Photography


Australia (Melbourne)

In Australia, I have captured the rugged coastline and the vast Outback of Perth, cityscape of Sydney, visited the Melbourne Great Ocean Road. I have photographed the Twelve Apostles at sunset and I also hiked through the Port Campbell National Park.


Landscape Photography


China (JuiZhaiGou)

In China, I travel to the mountainous area of JiuZhaiGou to see the spectacular colourful lakes and landscapes and visited the less travel part of Great Wall in Mutianyu in BeiJing. I have also hiked up to the summit to capture the sea of clouds in Er Mei San during the winter.


Landscape Photography


New Zealand (Queenstown)

In New Zealand, I have captured its dramatic landscapes and visited many scenic place that was feature in Lord of the Ring, like Arrow Town where the river flow through, Mount Cook and the most visited by tourist the Queenstown.


Landscape Photography


Japan (Osaka & Kyoto)


In Japan, where is I explored the country's stunning natural beauty and culture. I have capture the less travel place of Japan like the Kifune Shrine mountain walk and Minoh fall while also visited the famous Kiyomizu-dera, Fushimi Inari Taish, Nara Park and the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove while taking the scenic Sagano train ride.
