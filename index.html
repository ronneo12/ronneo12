Ron Neo


With over two and a half decades of experience, I offer a wealth of knowledge to contribute to any organization. I've held key roles as a Web & Design Project Manager in iMedia Center and Webmaster in the Corporate Communication Department at Ngee Ann Polytechnic. I have successfully led design teams for diverse web and print projects, amassing more than a decade of expertise in design, internet banking, and kiosk platforms.

I've cultivated a proven record of delivering high-quality functional designs within project timelines and budgets. My strengths include project management, team leadership, coding, and multimedia skills. As a Digital Art Director, UX/UI Designer, Illustrator, and Photographer, I've guided junior team members to infuse creativity into their work.

Passionate about design, I constantly explore new technologies and techniques to stay on the cutting edge. My commitment extends to attending design conferences, collaborating with peers, and continuous self-learning.
